# Reflection – picoCTF 2026

## Activity Description
I participated in picoCTF 2026 from March 9th to March 15th as an individual. I solved challenges across multiple systems, with a focus on general security skills, web exploitation, and some work in binary exploitation and forensics.

## Technical Decisions
I chose to focus on general skills and web exploitation because they gave strong foundational knowledge and practical experience. I used the Linux command line to work on challenges and used browser tools to analyze web-based challenges. When solving the problems, I focused on segmenting them into smaller steps and testing different approaches systematically to find the solution.

## Contributions
This was an individual activity. I solved 16 challenges and captured a screenshot as proof of completion.

## Quality Assessment
I would view my participation as effective. I solved challenges from multiple levels and developed stronger technical and analytical skills. One limitation was that I did not try any more advanced challenges. If I were to redo the event, I would work to higher-difficulty challenges to expand my knowledge base.

## Reflection & Professional Growth
This CTF strengthened my foundational skills, primarily in command-line usage, web security, and problem-solving. This experience aligns with my career goal of working in cybersecurity, and I plan to continue participating in CTF competitions.
